2016 Seattle Hmong New Year

Join us for our annual Hmong New Year celebration at the Seattle Center Armory on November 5th, 2016 from 11:00am-6:00pm! It will be an eventful day filled with cultural performances, food, activities, and more. Check out more below. For more questions/information, please email: hello@hmongofwa.org

INTERESTED PERFORMERS:

If you would like to perform on stage, please download and fill out the Performers Application 2016. NOTE: If you need any special equipment for your performance, please also fill out this Technical Information Sheet. Once completed, email BOTH of the completed application(s) to performers@hmongofwa.org Please contact geomoua@gmail.com or alicechang3@live.com if you have any other questions.
