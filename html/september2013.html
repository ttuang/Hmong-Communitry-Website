Monthly Archives: September 2013

Seattle Hmong New Year – November 9, 2013

The Hmong Association of Washington will hold the 2013/2014 Seattle Hmong New Year festival on November 9, 2013 at the Seattle Center Armory (305 Harrison Street Seattle, WA 98109). The event will consist of a day program followed by evening entertainment. Food, Traditional Hmong clothes, CDs/Videos, and other Hmong-related items will be on sale through vendors at the event. The Hmong Association of Washington is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. This year marks a huge milestone as it is HAW's 30th Anniversary!!! We are bringing in soloist Maiv Xyooj and Destiny Band to celebrate big with us! Seattle Hmong New Year November 9, 2013 11:00am-6:00pm Seattle Center Armory 305 Harrison St. Seattle, WA 98109 Evening Concert with Maiv Xyooj and Destiny Band November 9, 2013 7:00pm-11:00pm Fisher Pavilion.
